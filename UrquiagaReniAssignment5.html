Art Styles & Movements

Popular Art Styles

Art is a form of creative expression that is presented in many different forms. Like all forms of expressions, different styles and movements occur geographically or through creative influences. Some of the most famous artists have been credited with pioneering a certain style, and we as consumers of art are sometimes unaware of the names given to these particular styles. The following are some of the better known art styles and movements, after reading the short description, click on the link to view some of the better known art pieces of the particular style as well as some of the lesser known representations, along with a detailed description of the style.

a form of art created in one of the most explosive and creative moment of time in American history, the Harlem renaissance is a movement that has had a profound impact on African American culture and art. The migration of African Americans to the North from the South bought the art that would translate and show the African American life and identity. African American artists were encouraged to tell the story of their life through an African American point of view and shift away from the aesthetics of European and American artists.
